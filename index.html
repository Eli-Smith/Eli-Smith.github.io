About Me

Hey there, my name's Elijah and I started web development in 2019. I had never coded before, at the time my job just wasn't fulfilling and didn't offer me the opportunity to grow and learn that I wanted. I started simple, teaching myself what I could using online resources at home and practicing as much as possible. Eventually, as fate would have it, I heard about 1150 Academy a coding bootcamp that teaches its students how to code and prepares them for entry into the industry. In September 2019 I took the plunge, I left my job and went full time into the JavaScript course offered from 1150! It has been an amazing learning experience and challenged me to push myself in new ways. I'm excited to be starting this new career path and all the challenges and opportunities for growth and that come with it.
